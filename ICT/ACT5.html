B. Reflection

The most important thing I learned in this event is just how much effect appreciation can have to our teachers. I saw how happy our teachers became because of this party. That is why I should apply this knowledge and always say thank you. I should always be grateful in all the situations, such as when we are taught the lessons in school, and in many more.

In this event, I was one of the people that helped control the technology, such as the canva presentation. I also helped prepare the foods.

If I were to explain this topic to others, I would make them understand how hard our teachers work just to teach us. I will make them see the patience and effort that is given by our teachers. This way, they will understand why we should appreciate and be grateful to our teachers.

It is important that there is an event per subject so that we will really learn about these subjects and also have fun.

In this lesson, I learned about Cascading Style Sheets, its uses, and its benefits. I learned that it is used to style webpages, and describe how the elements will appear on the user's screen, and in other medias. I found out that it is beneficial, as it separates design from content, making maintenance easier. It also lets us style multiple webpages at once, saving a lot of work.
