B. Reflection

I think the most important thing I learned in this event is the appreciation of our own culture and also the culture of other Southeast-Asian countries. We should value our own country and its language because we are Filipinos. Likewise we should also respect the culture of other ASEAN nations. I will apply this learning to my every day life by speaking more in our own language and also by enjoying learning more about it. I am a proud Filipino. I will also learn more about the cultures of other countries in Southeast-Asia.

Some of the things I were able to participate in was the Philippine history test we took in the classroom. I also performed a deklamasyon in our classroom.

If I were to teach this event to others, the way I would explain it is that it is an event we do to appreciate our own country, and also the cultures of other Southeast-Asian nations. We do many activities in this month, such as the wearing of ASEAN attire, contests where we use the Filipino language, and many more.

Having an event like this for each subject is important so that the student will remember it. Thanks to the Buwan ng Wika, the students are still reminded to give value to their own language and culture. Through the various activities, we are able to practice using our own language.
