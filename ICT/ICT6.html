B. What I have Learned

In this lesson, I learned about the different CSS property for dimensions. This includes visibility, width, height, max-width, min-width, line-height, min-height, and max-height. I also learned about pseudo-classes, which are defined by a colon and is not user-defined. For hyperlinks, the pseudo-classes include link, visited, hover, and active.
